    About this atlas

    Every point is plotted from the public SSI and PADI dive-site locators. Only SSI publishes a logged-dive count per site, so all popularity rankings are SSI-only — PADI exposes no dive/visit metric, which is why the two are kept as separate, independently toggleable layers rather than merged.

    • SSI dive sites — sized & coloured by logged dives.
    • PADI dive sites — coloured by presence; some are unnamed map pins.
    • Centers — SSI carries full contact details; PADI carries membership tier, languages & activities.

    Built on MapLibre GL JS (globe projection) · MapTiler imagery · data harvested for educational use. Counts are point-in-time; logged dives tick up live at source.
